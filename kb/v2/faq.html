Account & Access

iTELL is best experienced using Google Chrome. While other modern browsers (Firefox, Edge) may work for basic functionality, some features like notes, highlights, and the AI chat are optimized for Chrome. Safari may have compatibility issues.

If you're part of a class, you must use the account provided by your instructor. Your class enrollment and progress tracking are tied to that specific email address. Using a different account will create a separate profile without class association.

Click on your profile icon in the top right corner and select "Sign Out" from the dropdown menu.

Summaries

Check the Details tab for specific feedback on which metrics need improvement. Common issues include: borrowing too much language from the text (paraphrase instead), missing main points (re-read the page), or writing too little/too much (aim for 100-150 words). If iTELL AI offers assisted re-reading, take advantage of it. See Tips for Better Summaries.

Summaries must be between 50 and 200 words. We recommend aiming for 100-150 words for the best results — enough to cover main points with supporting details without being overly verbose.

Go to your Dashboard and click the Summaries tab. You can filter by module and see which summaries passed or failed.

iTELL AI

Like all AI systems, iTELL AI can sometimes make mistakes. Always verify important information against the textbook itself. The AI is a learning aid, not a replacement for careful reading.

All conversations are recorded and stored on iTELL's servers for quality improvement purposes. The AI runs on iTELL's own infrastructure — your conversations are not sent to any third parties. See our Data & Privacy page for details.
