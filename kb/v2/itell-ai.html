What is iTELL AI?

iTELL AI is a conversational agent built into every page of the textbook. It can answer questions about the content you're reading, help you understand difficult concepts, and guide you through re-reading when your summary needs improvement.

Starting a Conversation

To start chatting with iTELL AI:

1

Open the chat

Click on the chat icon in the bottom right corner of your screen.

2

Type your question

Ask any question related to the textbook page you're currently reading.

3

Review the response

iTELL AI will provide an answer based on the textbook content. You can ask follow-up questions to deepen your understanding.

Ask AI (Highlight & Ask)

You can also ask iTELL AI to elaborate on specific parts of the text:

  1. Highlight text — Click and drag to select a passage you want explained.
  2. Click "Ask AI" — A button will appear near the selection.
  3. Read the explanation — iTELL AI will provide a detailed explanation of the highlighted passage.
Tip: Use "Ask AI" when you encounter unfamiliar terminology or complex concepts. The AI will break down the passage in simpler terms.

Assisted Re-reading

When your summary doesn't pass evaluation, iTELL AI may automatically guide you through a re-reading process:

1

Automatic redirect

After a failing summary, iTELL AI analyzes your submission and redirects you to the most relevant section of the page.

2

Re-read the highlighted section

Spend time carefully reading the highlighted text. This section was identified as important for improving your summary.

3

Answer the AI's question

When ready, click "I'm ready for question" and answer a comprehension question about the passage.

4

Return to your summary

Click "Return to summary" to go back and revise your summary with improved understanding.

iTELL AI will only automatically redirect you for re-reading once per page. After that, you can still use the chat to ask questions voluntarily.

Limitations & Notes

  • Like any AI, iTELL AI can make mistakes. Always double-check important information against the textbook.
  • iTELL AI's knowledge is limited to the textbook content — it may not be able to answer questions about outside topics.
  • All conversations with iTELL AI are recorded for quality improvement. Your conversations are not sent to third parties.
