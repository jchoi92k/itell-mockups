Accessing the Dashboard

To access your personal dashboard:

  1. Click on your profile icon at the top right of the screen.
  2. Select "Dashboard" from the dropdown menu.

Statistics Tab

The Statistics tab gives you an overview of your learning activity:

  • Progress — How many sections and pages you've completed.
  • Summary statistics — Number of summaries submitted vs. passed.
  • Daily reading time — A chart showing your reading activity over time.
  • Recent summaries — Quick links to your most recently written summaries.

Summaries Tab

The Summaries tab lets you browse all your past summary submissions:

  • Filter by module — Use the module selector to view summaries from specific chapters.
  • Pass/fail indicators — Each summary shows a blue checkmark (passed) or pink X (failed).
  • New summary — Click "New summary" to navigate to a specific section and write a new one.

Questions Tab

The Questions tab shows all Constructed Response Items you've answered:

  • Each row displays your answer to a question along with a score indicator (incorrect, partially correct, or correct).
  • Click on any row to navigate back to the relevant subsection in the textbook.
