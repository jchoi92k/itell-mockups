End-of-Page Quizzes

3 min read Last updated: March 2026 Mars only
This article applies to the Introduction to Criminal Justice (Mars) textbook. Other textbooks use different end-of-page activities.

What are end-of-page quizzes?

Some pages in your textbook end with a multiple-choice quiz. These quizzes test your comprehension of the material you just read and help reinforce key concepts.

How to complete a quiz

1

Finish reading the page

The quiz appears at the bottom of the page after you've scrolled through all content.

2

Answer each question

Select one answer per question. All questions must be answered before submitting.

3

Submit and review

Click "Submit Quiz" to see your results. Correct answers are highlighted in green, incorrect in red.

4

Proceed or retry

If you meet the passing threshold, you can move to the next page. Otherwise, review the material and try again.

Tip: Use the iTELL AI assistant to ask about concepts you're unsure about before retrying a quiz.
