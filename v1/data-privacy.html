Overview

iTELL collects usage data that is used internally to provide feedback and for systems improvement. Your data may be used for research purposes if you are part of a study and have consented for your data to be used for the specific purposes of the study.

We never share data with third-party companies.

Login Information

If you log in using single sign-on services (Google or Microsoft), iTELL collects the account information required for login authentication:

  • Name
  • Email address

This information is only used for user authentication purposes.

Usage Data

iTELL collects clickstream data to understand how you interact with the platform, including:

  • Clicks and scrolls
  • Interaction with page elements (e.g., clicking chunk reveal buttons, changing dashboard view settings)
  • Individual key presses in the summary activity textbox

Evaluation Data

iTELL stores all evaluations of:

  • User responses to Constructed Response Items
  • User-written summaries and their evaluation scores

AI Interaction Data

All conversations with iTELL AI are recorded. Important notes:

  • The AI is powered by an open-source model that runs on iTELL's own servers.
  • Your conversations are not sent to any third parties.

Third Parties

iTELL does not share your data with any third-party companies. Data is used solely for:

  1. Providing personalized feedback within the platform.
  2. Improving the iTELL system.
  3. Academic research (only with your explicit consent as part of a study).
