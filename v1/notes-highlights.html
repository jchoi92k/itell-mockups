Highlighting Text

You can highlight important passages for future reference:

  1. Select text — Click and drag through the text you want to highlight.
  2. Click "Highlight" — A toolbar appears near your selection.
  3. Done — The text block will be permanently highlighted with a colored background.

To remove a highlight, click on the highlighted text and select the delete option.

Creating Notes

1

Select text

Click and drag to select the passage you want to annotate.

2

Click "Note"

Choose the Note option from the toolbar that appears.

3

Write your note

A notepad opens on the right side of your screen. Type your note and click the return button to save.

The annotated text will change color to indicate it has an associated note. Your notes are visible in the right-side panel and accessible via the notes indicator at the top of the page.

Editing Notes & Colors

Click on any existing note to edit it. You can:

  • Change the color — Click the color selector to choose from the palette or enter a custom hex color code.
  • Edit the text — Click on the note content to modify it.
  • Delete — Remove the note entirely.

Known Limitations

Please be aware of these current limitations:
  • The note feature may not function properly when there are multiple notes on the same line.
  • The highlighting feature may not function properly when highlighting text across different subsections.
