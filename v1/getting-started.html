Overview

iTELL (Intelligent Texts for Enhanced Lifelong Learning) is a web application designed to transform your learning experience. It presents text-based learning materials alongside interactive videos, engaging learning activities, an AI assistant, and automated feedback.

Signing In

Follow these steps to create your account and sign in to iTELL.

1

Click Sign In

Click the Sign In button on the top right corner of your screen.

2

Choose your account

Log in using your Google or Microsoft account. If you are part of a class, use the account provided by your school.

3

Start learning

After signing in, you'll be taken to the textbook. Use the left-side navigation bar to access different pages and chapters.

If you sign in with the wrong account, sign out and try again with the correct one. Your class enrollment is tied to your specific email address.

Joining a Class

If your instructor has provided you with a class code or enrollment link, use the account they specified when signing in. Your progress and submissions will be visible to your instructor through the teacher dashboard.

Browser Requirements

iTELL works best with Google Chrome. While other modern browsers may work, we recommend Chrome for the optimal experience.

Some features (notes, highlights, AI chat) may not function correctly in Safari or older browsers. If you experience issues, try switching to Chrome.
